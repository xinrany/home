Exams

Midterms will be held during our usual class time. Each midterm will be timed for 45 minutes. No calculators are allowed for midterms or the final exam.

  • Midterm 1 will be held during the 5th week on Friday. It will cover Ch7.

    • There will be 6 questions on this exam.
    • Formulae to remember: substitution rule, integration by parts, trigonometric identity, double angle formulae, improper integrals: definition and the comparison test.

  • Midterm 2 will be held during the 8th week on Friday. It will cover Ch8-Ch11.4.

    • There will be 6 questions on this exam.
    • Formulae/examples to remember: arc length, surface of revolution, geometric series, harmonic series, the divergence test, the integral test and the comparison test.

  • Midterm 3 will be held during the 12th week on Friday. It will cover Ch11.
    • There will be 6 questions on this exam.
    • Formulae/definitions to remember: limit comparison test, alternating series test, the ratio and root tests, absolute vs. conditional convergence, radius and interval of convergence, the power series expansion of 1/1-x for |x| < 1.
  • Final exam will be held on May 9th, 1:30pm - 4:30pm at 9 FAR.
