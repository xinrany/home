List of materials to memorize

  • Chapter 7: Substitution rule, integration by parts, trigonometric identities, double-angle formulas, and improper integrals: their definitions and the comparison test.
  • Chapter 8: Arc length and surfaces of revolution.
  • Chapter 10: Parametrization of curves, arc length, and surfaces of revolution.
  • Chapter 11:
    • Geometric series and harmonic series.
    • Comparison tests for series: the divergence test, the integral test, the comparison test, the limit comparison test, the alternating series test, and the ratio and root tests.
    • Absolute convergence, conditional convergence, radius of convergence, and interval of convergence.
    • The power series expansion of \(\frac{1}{1-x}\) for \(|x| < 1\). Additionally, be familiar with Taylor's formula and some commonly used Maclaurin series.
