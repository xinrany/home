Spring 2025 & Fall 2024 IML

I'm continuing as the Graduate Mentor for the Illinois Mathematics Lab (IML) project: Historical Mathematical Models, in collaboration with the University of Illinois Library. This project catalogs part of an extensive collection of approximately 400 historical mathematical models—one of the largest in the world—previously housed in Altgeld Hall, and develops a comprehensive online exhibit. This semester, we are expanding the catalog and creating outreach materials, including 3D-printable models using Mathematica.

The Spring 2025 project builds on the Fall 2024 IML project: Documenting Historical Mathematical Models, where I also served as Graduate Mentor. Our team received the Nancy D. Anderson Undergraduate Research Award. For more information, visit here or view the poster below.
